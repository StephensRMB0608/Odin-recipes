Chicken Lasagna Recipe

Recipe Description

This is a classic homemade lasagna meal made with a meaty, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and very delicious cheesy fillings.

Total cooking time: 2 hr 50 mins

Servings: 20 Individuals

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomato
  • Parsley and garlic
  • spices and seasonings
  • sugar
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Three eggs

Steps:

  1. Cook the ground pork and beef with the diced onion until browned; add garlic, parsley, tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, sugar, and seasonings. Simmer 20 minutes.
  2. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan, eggs, and a pinch of seasoning to form the cheese layer.
  3. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, then layer noodles, cheese mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat until full; finish with mozzarella.
  4. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes.
  5. Uncover and bake 20–25 minutes more until the top is browned and bubbling; rest 10 minutes before slicing.

There you go. I really hope you enjoy my homemade lasgna with infinite deliciousness.
